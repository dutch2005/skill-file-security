## How it works

```
1.  npx @security-skill/cli
    в””в”Җ installs 29 security instruction files to .skills/security/
    в””в”Җ creates memory-security.md (tracks your score over time)
    в””в”Җ creates AI config files for every assistant on your machine
    в””в”Җ hardens .gitignore

2.  /security-scan
    в””в”Җ AI reads skill.md
    в””в”Җ auto-detects your stack (Next.js? Express? Docker? Firebase?)
    в””в”Җ runs the right checks for YOUR specific setup
    в””в”Җ gives you a prioritized list, most critical first

3.  /security-fix
    в””в”Җ shows you the diff
    в””в”Җ always asks before modifying anything
    в””в”Җ zero breaking changes guaranteed
```
